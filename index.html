Geoffrey A. H. Cochrane

DVM, MSc, DACAS Professional Animal Scientist (PAS)

Dr. Cochrane received his academic qualifications and formal education from the Ontario Veterinary College (DVM) and the Royal Veterinary College (MSc). In addition, a graduate diploma in business management (DBA) from the Faculty of Administration of the University of Ottawa was obtained. He is a Board Certified Diplomate in both Applied Animal Behavior and Animal Welfare with the American College of Animal Sciences, currently serving as Director of the Animal Welfare Science Discipline.


COMPANION ANIMALS

Small animal office practice limited to preventive and primary medical health care including:

  • Veterinary pediatrics – vaccinations, parasite prevention & behavioral consultations

  • Applied animal nutrition - body condition scoring, weight control, therapeutic dietary management

  • Veterinary geriatrics – inflammation & pain management, palliative care and quality of life assessment
